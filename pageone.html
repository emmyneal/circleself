time. passing.

cars on the street

inevitable movement in a place where It makes sense, even in places where It doesn’t

starting and stopping

but where is the start? the stop?

circle, oh how it all comes back to you

no beginning or end, no starting or stopping, in ten thousand places, yet nowhere all at the same time

going up, going down

elevators, bungee jumpers, the water cycle

man made, natural, simple, complex.

all of the above?

It makes sense even where It doesn’t.
